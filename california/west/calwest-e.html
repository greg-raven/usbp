West California Part 5

Not all the threats are from MS-13 gang members or the drug cartels. Even the Russians stick their noses over the border.

Just before reaching the massive Port of Entry and its daily stream of 40,000 cars traversing 23 inspection lanes, we have the former KGB spy tower looking at the United States Fleet not ten miles away. The KGB is gone but the FSB is alive and busily peeking.

As the border barrier continues eastward the City of Tijuana is cut in two by the Tijuana River Flood Channel. The Primary barrier can be seen as the rust colored line, the continuing yellow line along the bottom of the concrete channel and then starting again on the far east side.
