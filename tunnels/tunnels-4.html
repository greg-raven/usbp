Border Tunnels Part 4

Thanks to our ever so efficient and understanding politicians — and this new law — the drug cartels have started thinking. There are all sorts of ways to build a tunnel that can be even 1,000 feet long and yet cheap and easy to build.

New tunnels of this type do pose problems for the drug cartels but these criminal organizations have overcome many problems in the past.

While slowly passing Afghani terrorists through a one inch diameter tube might be our constant dream, cocaine and heroin and methamphetamine and even marijuana can be blasted through such tubes quite easily.

Can even these new one or two inch diameter tunnels be detected? Sure, they are easy to find. Will anyone actually take steps to protect us?

Probably not.
