Central Arizona Part 4

This article in a major U.S. newspaper relates a story almost opposite the sworn testimony to Congress. In the sworn testimony to Congress they are begging for help to stop the violence, the murders, the dumping of 4,300 stolen cars on the reservation in one year, 55,000 illegal aliens captured on the reservation by Federal Agents in one year, and that even their elderly don’t dare walk out their front doors fear for their safety. Here, the press says the tribe doesn’t want protection. Who’s spinning? While the border paper ran one story, the publisher’s reporters, who covered the sworn testimoney before congress, offered a different story.

Even USA Today lays it all on the line but a border paper hides the truth.
